2024 Lok Sabha elections: Why Congress strategy with focus on Karnataka could fail


Bulletin.com | December 14-2023

      
NEW DELHI: The Congress on Wednesday said that Karnataka wil be at the centre of the grand old party's
election strategy  or the upcoming Lok Sabha elections. The party also announceced that it will soon start the process of selecting candidates for the parliamentary elections.
"We believe that the people of Karnataka are going to bless the Congress with more than 20 seats in the Lok Sabha polls.We hope that this number could go even beyond 25 and we would be 
able to sweep the polls in the state," Congress leader Randeep Surjewala said.The Congress's enthusiasm and optimism about possible Lok Sabha sweep in Karnataka stems from the grand success
 the party achieved in the state assembly elections held earlier this year. In the 2023 assembly polls, the Congress with 135 seats decimated the BJP toreturn to power in the state. The 
 saffron party was reduced to 66 seats. The other key player in the state politics, the JD(S) of former Prime Minister H D Deve Gowda, also fared poorly and was reduced to 19 seats from the 37 it held in 2018
 However, the Congress leaders and planners would do well to remember that in 2013 also the grand old party had fared wel in the assembly elections winning 122 seats against the BJP' 40. However, this convincing 
 victory in the state polls had little impact on the national elections that followed. In the 2014 Lok Sabha elections, the BJP won 17 seats with a vote share of 43.37% while the Congress could manage only 9 with 
 41.2% vote share.
